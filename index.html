Where's home?

An exploration of my geographical past (and of the Google Maps API).

Millington, MD

I grew up on a farm (all crops, no animals...sorry) on the Eastern Shore of Maryland.

Chapel Hill, NC

I'm a proud Tar Heel! I chose Carolina for its strong academics across the board (I had no idea what I wanted to study when I was a first-year), school spirit and affordability.

Winchester, VA

In the summer after my sophomore year I worked as an actor for Shenandoah Summer Music Theatre. I had a blast and got live out one of my lifelong dreams - plus I lived next to a Sheetz for two whole months!

Quito, Pichincha, Ecuador

In the summer of 2015 I studied abroad right in the heart of Quito. Ecuador is an incredible place, but studying abroad is not for the faint of heart. Despite its ups and downs, I still hold this place in my tiny American heart.
